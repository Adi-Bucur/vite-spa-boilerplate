Frequently Asked Questions

The NeuroLink Pro is completely non-invasive and uses the same technology as medical-grade EEG devices. It only reads electrical signals from the surface of your scalp - no signals are sent to your brain. The device is FDA-approved and meets all international safety standards.

Initial setup takes about 15 minutes, including device pairing and basic calibration. Our AI learns your unique brain patterns over the first few sessions, with optimal performance typically achieved within 2-3 hours of use.

NeuroLink Pro works with Windows 10+, macOS 10.15+, Linux (Ubuntu 20.04+), iOS 14+, and Android 10+. It connects via Bluetooth 5.2 or Wi-Fi 6, and we provide native apps, web interfaces, and developer APIs.

The device is safe for most users, but we recommend consulting with your healthcare provider if you have epilepsy, implanted medical devices (pacemakers, etc.), or active neurological conditions. Our medical team can provide specific guidance based on your situation.

Your NeuroLink Pro package includes: the main device, premium carrying case, wireless charging dock, quick-start guide, 1-year warranty, priority customer support, and lifetime access to software updates and new features.
